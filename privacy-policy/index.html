Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

Zaza Apps ("we", "our", or "us") operates the VoxBox Audio Mixer mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide

  • Audio files — Audio recordings and files you import or create within the App. These are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly use a cloud-based feature (e.g., AI audio tools).
  • Projects — Your mixer projects and settings are stored locally on your device.

b) Automatically Collected Information

  • Device identifier — A unique device ID used to manage your subscription and purchase status.
  • Usage analytics — Anonymous usage data such as feature usage, session duration, and app events to help us improve the App.
  • Crash reports — Diagnostic data to identify and fix bugs.

c) Purchase Information

  • Subscription and in-app purchase records (product ID, purchase token, timestamps) are stored securely via Google Firebase to verify your premium status across sessions.
  • We do not collect or store your payment card details. All payments are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality.
  • To verify and manage your subscription or purchase status.
  • To deliver AI-powered features (text-to-speech, audio separation) when you choose to use them.
  • To display relevant advertisements (for free-tier users).
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the App.
  • To respond to support requests.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — Analytics, crash reporting, and cloud storage.
  • Google AdMob — Advertising (free-tier users only).
  • Apple / Google Play Billing — In-app purchases and subscriptions.
  • OpenAI / ElevenLabs — AI audio processing features (data is sent only when you explicitly use these features).

4. Data Storage and Security

Your audio files and projects are stored locally on your device. Purchase verification data is stored securely on Google Firebase with access controls. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, but no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you delete the App or clear its data. Purchase records in Firebase are retained as long as needed to verify your subscription status.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Delete your local app data at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing its storage.
  • Request deletion of your purchase records from our servers by contacting us.
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
